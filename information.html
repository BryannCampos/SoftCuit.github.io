SoftCuit is created to combat and prevent failures in technological security systems for the Panamanian population that has digital devices. SoftCuit is an application designed to protect your data, providing the user with the alternative of synchronising applications while taking care of the different sites when surfing the web. In SoftCuit you will also have the option to download the files of your choice in a secure way.
